About Legum Festum

Legum Festum is the premier annual social and cultural festival of our esteemed law school. It is a vibrant celebration that brings together students, faculty, and legal professionals for a confluence of culture, competition, and camaraderie. More than just a festival, it's a showcase of talent, a battle of wits, and a platform for forging lasting bonds.

Each year, we set the stage for an unforgettable experience, blending traditional cultural performances with intellectually stimulating legal competitions. From the intense Moot Court sessions and heated debates to mesmerizing dance performances and soulful musical nights, Legum Festum is an event that embodies the spirit of our institution and the pursuit of excellence. Join us as we create history and celebrate the perfect synergy of law and life.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the dates for Legum Festum?

Legum Festum is scheduled to take place from November 10th to November 14th, 2025.

How can I register for events?

You can register for individual events by clicking on the "Register Now" button on the respective event's page. The last date for registration is November 5th, 2025.

Is accommodation provided for participants?

Yes, accommodation for outstation participants will be provided. Details regarding the accommodation will be sent to registered participants via email.

Are there any registration fees?

Yes, there is a nominal registration fee for each event. The specific fee for each event is mentioned on its detail page.
